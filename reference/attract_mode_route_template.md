# Pac-Man Attract Mode Demo Route

Walk Pac through the maze below by replacing the dots (`.`),
power pellets (`o`), and empty floor (` `) along his path with
the direction letter:

- `L` = Pac is moving LEFT through this tile
- `R` = Pac is moving RIGHT
- `U` = Pac is moving UP
- `D` = Pac is moving DOWN

Mark every tile Pac visits (including straight-through tiles, not
just the turn points). I'll extract turn waypoints from the change
points and double-check no marked tile is a wall.

Legend:
- `#` = wall (don't touch)
- `.` = floor with dot pellet
- `o` = floor with energiser (power pellet)
- ` ` = floor without pellet (e.g. tunnel row, ghost pen, junction)
- `S` = Pac-Man spawn (our col=14, row=23). Replace with the
       direction Pac heads OUT (the arcade demo starts going LEFT).

Notes:
- Row 14 is the tunnel (the only row where col 0 and col 27 are walkable).
- Energisers (`o`) are at (1,3), (26,3), (1,23), (26,23).
- The ghost pen door is on row 11 between cols 13 and 14. Pac never enters.
- Column index runs 0..27 left to right; row index runs 0..30 top to bottom.
